Competition

Small Opportunities are often the beginning of great enterprises. Therefore competions are conducted on various occasion events like Action songs, Story Telling, Colouring, Creative Drawing and Vegetable Carving. Assessment Toppers are rewarded with the Rolling Trophy for scoring the maximum marks.

Early Start Programme

A programme for children aged 2.5 years and over, where they are prepared for school. Children learn pre-reading, pre-writing skills, listen to stories, painting and play.
